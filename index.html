O.B Montessori Center

Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center offers a learning environment that blends structure, creativity, and independence to develop well-rounded students. Guided by Montessori principles, the school emphasizes purposeful work, exploration, and character formation.

What is Montessori Learning?

Montessori education is a child-centered approach emphasizing independence, self-directed learning, and hands-on activities within a specially prepared environment.
